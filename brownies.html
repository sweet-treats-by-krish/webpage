Our fudgy, decadent Brownies are made with premium dark chocolate and real butter for an intense chocolate experience. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of rich chocolate flavor with a slightly crisp top and dense, fudgy center.

Category: Confectionery
Size: 8x8-inch pan (9 large brownies)
Ingredients: Dark chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, flour, cocoa powder, vanilla
Allergens: Contains gluten, dairy, eggs
Shelf Life: 5 days at room temperature, 2 weeks refrigerated

Product Details

Our brownies are made with a special blend of high-quality dark chocolate and cocoa powder for maximum chocolate flavor. The recipe has been perfected to achieve the ideal fudgy texture - dense and moist without being underbaked. The top develops a shiny, crackly crust that contrasts beautifully with the rich interior.

Available plain or with optional add-ins. For a special treat, try warming a brownie for 10 seconds in the microwave and serving with vanilla ice cream.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve warm with vanilla ice cream and chocolate sauce
  • Cut into small squares for a dessert platter
  • Crumble over yogurt or oatmeal for breakfast
  • Use as a base for brownie sundaes with your favorite toppings
  • Pair with coffee or milk for an afternoon treat
