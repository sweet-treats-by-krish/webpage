Our Business

Our Story

Sweet Treats by Krish began in December 10, 2018 as a small home-based bakery with a passion for creating delicious desserts. What started as weekend hobby for founder Krishna Tiquia quickly blossomed into a beloved local business.

Our Mission

At Sweet Treats by Krish, our mission is to create moments of joy through our handcrafted confections. We believe that quality ingredients, traditional techniques, and a dash of innovation are the perfect recipe for unforgettable treats.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our ingredients or our products. Every treat that leaves our kitchen meets our high standards of excellence.

Innovation

While we respect traditional recipes, we also love to experiment with new flavors and techniques to create unique and exciting treats.

Community

We're proud to be part of our local community and strive to give back through various initiatives and partnerships with local organizations.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact by using sustainable packaging and sourcing local ingredients whenever possible.

Our Products

Product Materials

We believe that the best treats start with the best ingredients. That's why we source high-quality, fresh ingredients from trusted suppliers. Whenever possible, we use locally sourced, organic ingredients to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

Preparation Process

Each of our products is handcrafted with care in small batches to ensure quality and consistency. Our skilled bakers and pastry chefs follow traditional techniques while incorporating modern innovations to create the perfect balance of flavor and texture.

Our preparation process includes:

  • Daily fresh baking with no pre-made mixes
  • Traditional fermentation for our bread and pastries
  • Hand-decorating each cake and confection
  • Strict quality control checks before any product leaves our kitchen
  • Careful packaging to maintain freshness and presentation

Shipping Information

We take great care to ensure that our products reach you in perfect condition. Our shipping and delivery methods are designed to maintain the quality, freshness, and appearance of our treats from our kitchen to your door.

Shipping details:

  • Local delivery available within a 10-mile radius of our store
  • Eco-friendly packaging materials
  • Express shipping options available for urgent orders
  • Tracking information provided for all shipped orders
