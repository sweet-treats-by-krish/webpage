Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How can I update my account information?

After logging in, go to your account dashboard and click on "Profile" or "Account Settings." From there, you can update your personal information, change your password, and manage your preferences.

Can I use social media accounts to sign in?

Yes, you can use your Google or Facebook account to sign in. This makes the login process quicker and eliminates the need to remember another password. Your account will be linked to your email address.

How do I delete my account?

If you wish to delete your account, please contact our customer support team at info@sweettreatsbykrish.com. Please note that deleting your account will remove all your saved information, including order history and saved addresses.
