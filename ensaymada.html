Our Premium Ensaymada is a Filipino classic made extraordinary. This soft, buttery brioche is topped with creamy buttercream, grated cheese, and sugar for the perfect balance of sweet and savory. Each bite is light, fluffy, and absolutely irresistible.

Category: Pastry

Allergens: Contains gluten, dairy, eggs

Product Details

Our Premium Ensaymada is made with a special brioche dough that's enriched with butter and eggs for a tender, fluffy texture. The dough undergoes a slow fermentation process to develop flavor before being shaped into spirals and baked to golden perfection. Each ensaymada is generously topped with our house-made buttercream, a dusting of sugar, and a sprinkle of grated cheese.

We use only premium ingredients, including European-style butter and high-quality cheese, to create an ensaymada that's superior to commercial versions. The result is a pastry that's rich but not heavy, sweet but balanced by the savory cheese.

The History of Ensaymada

Ensaymada traces its origins to the Spanish "ensaimada," a spiral-shaped pastry from Mallorca. When the Spanish brought it to the Philippines during colonial times, it evolved to suit local tastes, becoming sweeter and richer with the addition of butter, sugar, and cheese toppings.

Traditionally served during special occasions and holidays, ensaymada has become a beloved everyday treat in Filipino culture. Our version honors this tradition while using premium ingredients and techniques to elevate the classic recipe.
