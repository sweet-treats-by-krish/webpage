Our famous Cinnamon Rolls are made with a rich, buttery dough, filled with cinnamon sugar and crunchy walnuts, then topped with cream cheese icing. These indulgent treats are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or dessert.

Category: Pastry
Size: 6 large rolls per pan
Ingredients: Flour, butter, milk, sugar, cinnamon, walnuts, cream cheese, vanilla
Allergens: Contains gluten, dairy, eggs, tree nuts
Shelf Life: 2 days at room temperature, 5 days refrigerated

Product Details

Our Cinnamon Rolls are made with a special brioche-style dough that's rich and tender. The filling features premium cinnamon and brown sugar, plus crunchy walnuts for texture contrast. Each roll is generously topped with our signature cream cheese icing that perfectly balances the sweetness.

Available with or without walnuts upon request. For special occasions, we can add raisins or other dried fruits to the filling. These rolls freeze well - simply thaw and warm before serving.

Heating Instructions

  • Oven: Preheat to 350Â°F. Place rolls on baking sheet and cover loosely with foil. Heat for 10-12 minutes until warm throughout.
  • Microwave: Place roll on microwave-safe plate. Heat for 15-20 seconds. Be careful not to overheat as icing may melt.
  • From Frozen: Thaw overnight in refrigerator. Warm as above or microwave for 30-45 seconds.
