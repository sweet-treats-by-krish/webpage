Questions about Products

Are your products made fresh daily?
Yes, all our products are freshly made each day. We believe in delivering the freshest and highest quality treats to our customers.
Do you offer gluten-free options?
Yes, we have a selection of gluten-free products. These are prepared in a separate area to avoid cross-contamination. Please check our Products page for our current gluten-free offerings.
What ingredients do you use in your products?
We use high-quality, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. Detailed ingredient information is available for each product on our website, or you can ask our staff in-store.
Can I customize my cake or pastry order?
Absolutely! We offer customization options for many of our products. You can specify flavors, decorations, and sizes. For custom orders, please use our request form or contact us directly.
How long do your products stay fresh?
Our products are best enjoyed within 1-3 days of purchase, depending on the item. Storage recommendations are provided with each purchase to ensure maximum freshness.

Questions about Services

Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer delivery within a 10-mile radius of our store. Delivery fees vary based on distance. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 10 AM.
Can you cater for events and parties?
Yes, we offer catering services for various events including birthdays, weddings, corporate events, and more. Please contact us at least one week in advance for catering requests.
Do you offer gift packaging?
We offer various gift packaging options for our products, from simple gift boxes to elaborate hampers. Additional charges may apply depending on the packaging chosen.
How far in advance should I place my order?
For regular orders, 24-48 hours notice is appreciated. For custom or large orders, we recommend placing your order at least one week in advance. During holiday seasons, earlier ordering is recommended.

Miscellaneous Questions

What are your store hours?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 7 AM to 7 PM. Hours may vary during holidays, so please check our social media or call ahead.
Do you have a loyalty program?
Currently, we do not have any loyalty programs. However, we are finding ways to implement one depending on future endeavors!
Are there any discounts for bulk orders?
Depending on the orders and situation!
Do you ship your products nationally?
Currently, we only ship certain shelf-stable products in Luzon. Most of our fresh baked goods are available for local delivery or pickup only.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash and mobile payment options like PayMaya and GCash. For online orders, we also accept PayPal.
