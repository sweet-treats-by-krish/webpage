Our Brigadeiro Cake brings the authentic taste of Brazil to your table. This rich chocolate cake is filled and covered with traditional brigadeiro (Brazilian chocolate fudge), then decorated with chocolate sprinkles for an irresistible treat.

Category: Confectionery
Size: 6-inch round (serves 4-6)
Ingredients: Chocolate, condensed milk, butter, cocoa powder, flour, eggs, sugar
Allergens: Contains gluten, dairy, eggs
Shelf Life: 3 days refrigerated

Product Details

Our Brigadeiro Cake features layers of moist chocolate cake alternating with thick layers of authentic Brazilian brigadeiro (a fudgy chocolate mixture made with condensed milk). The entire cake is covered in more brigadeiro and decorated with traditional chocolate sprinkles.

This cake is rich and indulgent - a small slice goes a long way! Perfect for celebrations or as a special treat. For best results, let the cake sit at room temperature for 30 minutes before serving to allow the brigadeiro to soften slightly.

The Brigadeiro Story

Brigadeiro is Brazil's most beloved sweet treat, created in the 1940s. The name comes from Brigadier Eduardo Gomes, a presidential candidate whose supporters sold these chocolate candies as a campaign fundraiser.

Traditional brigadeiro is made by cooking condensed milk with cocoa powder and butter, then rolling the mixture into balls covered in chocolate sprinkles. Our cake version brings this classic flavor to a whole new level with layers of cake and generous amounts of brigadeiro filling and frosting.
