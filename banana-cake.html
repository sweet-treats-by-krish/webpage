Our moist and flavorful Banana Cake is made with ripe bananas and a hint of cinnamon for the perfect balance of sweetness and spice. Topped with cream cheese frosting and garnished with fresh banana slices, this cake is a crowd favorite.

Category: Pastry
Size: 8-inch round or 9x13-inch rectangular
Ingredients: Ripe bananas, flour, sugar, eggs, butter, cinnamon, cream cheese, vanilla
Allergens: Contains gluten, dairy, eggs
Shelf Life: 3 days at room temperature, 5 days refrigerated

Product Details

Our Banana Cake is made with the finest ingredients, including locally sourced ripe bananas that are mashed by hand to preserve texture. The cake is baked to perfection with a moist crumb and topped with our signature cream cheese frosting that's not too sweet, allowing the banana flavor to shine through.

Perfect for birthdays, gatherings, or as a special treat for yourself. Can be customized with nuts (walnuts or pecans) upon request. For best results, store at room temperature if consuming within 3 days, or refrigerate for longer storage.
