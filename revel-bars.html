Our Revel Bars feature a buttery oatmeal cookie base layered with rich chocolate fudge, then topped with more oatmeal cookie dough. These bars offer the perfect combination of chewy, crunchy, and fudgy textures in every bite.

Category: Confectionery
Size: 9x13-inch pan (12 large bars)
Ingredients: Oats, flour, butter, brown sugar, condensed milk, chocolate, eggs, vanilla
Allergens: Contains gluten, dairy, eggs
Shelf Life: 5 days at room temperature, 2 weeks refrigerated

Product Details

Our Revel Bars are made with a thick layer of homemade chocolate fudge sandwiched between two layers of chewy oatmeal cookie dough. The oatmeal cookie provides texture and balances the sweetness of the chocolate, while the fudge layer stays soft and creamy.

These bars are perfect for sharing at gatherings, packing in lunches, or enjoying as an afternoon treat with coffee or milk. They freeze exceptionally well - simply thaw at room temperature when ready to enjoy.

The Origin of Revel Bars

Revel Bars are believed to have originated in the American Midwest, where oatmeal and chocolate were pantry staples. The name "Revel" comes from the word "revelation," as the combination of flavors was considered revolutionary when first created.

Our version stays true to the classic recipe while using premium ingredients for superior flavor. The oatmeal cookie base is made with rolled oats for maximum texture, and the chocolate fudge is cooked slowly to develop deep chocolate flavor.
