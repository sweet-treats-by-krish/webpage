Product Details

Our Korean Cream Cheese Garlic Bread starts with soft, pillowy milk bread that's cut into wedges but kept connected at the base. Each piece is generously filled with our sweet cream cheese mixture, then dipped in a luxurious garlic butter sauce made with real garlic, butter, and a touch of condensed milk for sweetness.

The bread is then baked until the exterior becomes slightly crisp while the inside remains soft and gooey. Finished with a sprinkle of parsley, these addictive treats offer the perfect balance of sweet cream cheese and savory garlic flavors.

The Korean Garlic Bread Phenomenon

Korean Cream Cheese Garlic Bread became a viral sensation in South Korea before spreading worldwide. Originating from small bakeries in Seoul, this innovative take on garlic bread combines Western and Korean baking techniques.

What sets it apart is the sweet cream cheese filling that contrasts beautifully with the garlic butter coating. The bread's unique flower-like shape isn't just for looks - it creates more surface area for the delicious garlic butter to coat.
