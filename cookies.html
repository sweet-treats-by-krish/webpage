Our classic Chocolate Chip Cookies are crispy on the edges, chewy in the center, and loaded with premium chocolate chips. Made with real butter and brown sugar for that perfect caramelized flavor everyone loves.

Category: Confectionery
Size Options: Small (12 cookies), Large (24 cookies)
Ingredients: Flour, butter, brown sugar, eggs, chocolate chips, vanilla, baking soda, salt
Allergens: Contains gluten, dairy, eggs
Shelf Life: 1 week at room temperature, 2 weeks refrigerated

Product Details

Our Chocolate Chip Cookies are made with a special blend of semi-sweet and milk chocolate chips for balanced sweetness. The dough is chilled for 24 hours before baking to develop flavor and ensure perfect texture. Each cookie is hand-scooped for that homemade look and feel.

Available in two sizes and three doneness levels to suit your preference. Can be customized with nuts or other mix-ins upon request (additional charge may apply).

Storage Tips

  • Room Temperature: Store in an airtight container with a slice of bread to maintain softness (replace bread daily).
  • Refrigerator: Place in airtight container or ziplock bag for longer storage.
  • Freezer: Cookies freeze well for up to 3 months. Thaw at room temperature or warm briefly in oven.
  • Reviving Stale Cookies: Place in microwave with a damp paper towel for 10 seconds to restore softness.
